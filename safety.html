Safety

At EcoRide, your safety is our top priority. We are committed to providing a safe and secure ride experience for all our users. Here are some of the safety measures we have in place:

  • All drivers undergo thorough background checks.
  • Real-time GPS tracking of rides.
  • In-app emergency button for immediate assistance.
  • 24/7 customer support for any safety concerns.
